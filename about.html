Who We Are

The Vaccination Tracker project is built to help individuals and communities manage their health records more effectively. By providing easy-to-use tools, we empower families to stay on top of their vaccination schedules.

Our Mission

To simplify vaccination management, raise awareness, and ensure communities are healthier and protected from preventable diseases.

Our Vision

A world where healthcare access is equitable, and every individual is empowered to take control of their health journey.

Our Team

A dedicated group of medical professionals and software developers working together to bridge technology and healthcare.
