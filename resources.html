Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is a vaccine?

A vaccine is a biological preparation that provides active acquired immunity to a particular infectious disease.

Are vaccines safe?

Yes, vaccines undergo rigorous testing and monitoring by health authorities to ensure their safety and effectiveness.

Why should I get vaccinated?

Vaccination protects you from serious diseases and helps achieve 'herd immunity,' protecting those who cannot be vaccinated.
